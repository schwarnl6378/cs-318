My Favorite Recipe

Burger King Comfort Food

Ingredients

  • Smartphone with the Burger King app installed
  • A car (preferably a 2013 Scion xB)
  • At least $10 in your checking account
  • A decent-sized appetite
  • Untreated Depression (optional)

Instructions

  1. Open the Burger King app on your smartphone
  2. Add a Double Cheeseburger with only ketchup to your order
  3. Add a Large Onion Rings to your order
  4. Add an 8-pc Chicken Nuggets to your order
  5. Place your order and select your payment method
  6. Go out to your car and drive to your local Burger King restaurant
  7. Pull into the drive-thru and tell them that you have a mobile order for (your name here)
  8. Pull around to the window
  9. Silently commiserate with the employee who's just as over it all as you are
  10. Softly utter a "thank you" when they hand you your order
  11. Drive back home
  12. Turn on a fictional TV program of your choice
  13. Eat
  14. Wonder where it all went so wrong
